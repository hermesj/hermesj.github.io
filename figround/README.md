# Figure and Ground / Figur und Hintergrund

A literary project: two parallel prose texts on the myth of Odysseus and the Siren, presented as an interleaved visual poster, a text overlay (focus version) and audio overlay (audio version).

- [Demo](https://hermesj.github.io/figround/)
- [Overview](index.html)
- [Poster (Deutsch)](poster.html?lang=de) — „Figur und Hintergrund", das Poster auf Deutsch
- [Poster (English)](poster.html?lang=en) — „Figure and Ground", the poster in English
- [Focus](focus.html) – Interactive reading view — gyroscope, touch, or mouse control the focus and visibility of both texts (de/en).
- [Alignment](alignment.html) — both texts on independent prompter bands; drag, swipe, or use the wheel (de/en).
- [Palimpsest](palimpsest.html) — one text layered over the other; swap and scroll to compare (de/en).
- [Audio](audio.html) — experimental audio version with crossfade between two voices (de/en) – work in progress
