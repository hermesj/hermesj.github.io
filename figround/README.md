# Figure and Ground / Figur und Hintergrund

A literary project: two parallel prose texts on the myth of Odysseus and the Siren, presented as an interleaved visual poster.

- [Figur und Hintergrund](figur-hintergrund.html) — the original German text
- [Figure and Ground](figure-ground.html) — an independent English rendering, not a translation
